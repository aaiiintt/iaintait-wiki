# Derek McKenna

## Overview

Derek McKenna was the Lead Flash Developer at POKE London during the Orange digital campaigns era. His LinkedIn profile (partially accessible) confirms: "Lead Flash Developer on award winning projects including 'The Never Ending Website' (Orange Unlimited)."

He is also named in the Creative Bloq 2009 profile of Iain Tait as a lead developer on Orange Balloonacy.
