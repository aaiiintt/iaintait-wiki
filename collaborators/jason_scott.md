# Jason Scott

## Overview

Jason Scott was a copywriter at W+K London, working as part of the "Jason and Joris" creative pairing with art director Joris Philippart. The two worked under ECDs Iain Tait and Tony Davidson across multiple campaigns.

## Key Projects

- **[WWF Endangered Emojis](../projects/wk_wwf_endangered_emojis.md)** — Copywriter (2015). The campaign generated 559,000 Twitter mentions and 59,000 registrations in its first months and was named one of Marketing Week's top campaigns of 2015.
