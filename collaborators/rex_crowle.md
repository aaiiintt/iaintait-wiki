# Rex Crowle

## Overview

Rex Crowle (known professionally as Rexbox) is a British animator and illustrator. He was directly named by Nik Roope in the Dezeen launch coverage for Orange Unlimited (November 2007): *"We worked with an animator called Rex..."* He was responsible for the character animation and illustrated content in the never-ending webpage.

## Key Projects

- **[Orange Unlimited](../projects/poke_orange_unlimited.md)** — Animator / Illustrator (2007). Named by Nik Roope in Dezeen; listed in Campaign Annual 2007 creatives.
