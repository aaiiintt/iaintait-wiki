# Freddie Powell

Creative director turned commercials director; co-founded London production company DROOL in 2020 alongside Genevieve Sheppard after a long stint at Wieden+Kennedy, where he held a creative director role ([DROOL](https://www.droolprods.com/about); [The Drum, 2015](https://www.thedrum.com/news/2015/05/15/dreamers-wanted-wieden-kennedy-s-freddie-powell-writes-creative-future)). At W+K London he worked in a long-standing creative partnership with Hollie Walker on brands including TK Maxx and Lurpak ([Behance: Freddie & Hollie](https://www.behance.net/freddieandhollie)).
