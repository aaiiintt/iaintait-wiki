# Matt Clark

## Overview

Matt Clark is a prominent creative entrepreneur, business advisor, and partner at **[FOOD](../agencies/food.md)**. With a deep background in scaling creative enterprises, Matt is a partner at Mother and Mother Ventures (note: these external entities do not have profiles in this wiki; do not link to them), and was instrumental in Mother's support of POKE during its foundational early years. He brings extensive commercial intelligence, business strategy, and entrepreneurial leadership to the FOOD partnership.

## Career Highlights

* **POKE Support**: Key figure at Mother and Mother Ventures who championed and facilitated Mother's strategic backing and support of POKE in its early Shoreditch era.
* **Mother & Mother Ventures**: Partner, providing business guidance, incubation strategy, and backing for innovative creative startups.
* **FOOD**: Partner, joining Nick Farnhill, Iain Tait, and Richard Turley to guide the consultancy's commercial growth, joint ventures, and strategic partnerships.

## Background & Expertise

Matt is recognized as a key business brain and venture incubator within the creative industries. His expertise spans brand strategy, venture modeling, investment alignment, and advisory for high-impact creative and technology consultancies.
