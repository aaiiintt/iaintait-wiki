# Joe De Souza

Group Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy London whose client work spans Kraft Heinz, Arla, Coca-Cola, Honda and Sainsbury's; previously held senior creative roles at Mother London, Wieden+Kennedy Amsterdam, Fallon London and Karmarama. Awards include a D&AD Black Pencil for Writing and a 2025 Cannes Gold Lion. *(sources: [joedesouza.com/about](https://joedesouza.com/about), [LinkedIn](https://uk.linkedin.com/in/joe-de-souza-39166b8), [Synima interview](https://www.synima.com/joe-de-souza-on-leadership-that-inspires-excellence/))*
