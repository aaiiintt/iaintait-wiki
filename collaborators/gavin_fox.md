# Gavin Fox

## Overview

Gavin Fox was a Creative Director at POKE London, confirmed there from 2009 through at least 2013. He was one of the lead creatives on the Oasis Rubberduckzilla AR campaign (2009) working under ECD Iain Tait.

After POKE, he joined Framestore's immersive department in 2014, going on to win major industry awards for theme park/immersive experience work: the Lionsgate Entertainment World "Midnight Ride" (Thea Award, 2021), Jurassic World Adventure (VES Award, 2022), and Gardaland Jumanji The Adventure (VES nominated, 2023).
