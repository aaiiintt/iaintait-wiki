# Gretel

Independent branding, strategy and design studio based in Brooklyn, NY, founded in 2005 by Greg Hahn (principals Greg Hahn and Ryan Moore). Gretel partnered with Google Creative Lab on the Chromebook "For Everyone" launch, including the Times Square takeover that staged a holiday-weekend takeover of 9 of Times Square's biggest screens with user-submitted "For…" images.
