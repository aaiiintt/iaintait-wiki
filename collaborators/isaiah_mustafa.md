# Isaiah Mustafa

**Project:** Old Spice — "The Man Your Man Could Smell Like" and Responses Campaign (2010)

## Overview

Isaiah Mustafa is an American actor and former NFL wide receiver who became the face of Old Spice as "The Old Spice Guy." He starred in the original Super Bowl spot "The Man Your Man Could Smell Like" (directed at the Cannes Grand Prix — Film) and remained in character and on set throughout the 2.5-day production of the Responses campaign, filming 186 personalised video responses to fans, celebrities, and internet personalities.

His performance — maintaining character while delivering bespoke, rapid-fire responses to targets including Demi Moore, Ellen DeGeneres, and Twitter co-founder Biz Stone — was central to the campaign's success.
