# Dan Wieden

Co-founder of [Wieden+Kennedy](../agencies/wieden_and_kennedy_portland.md) (W+K) and the agency's long-time creative leader. He led W+K during [Iain Tait's](../collaborators/iain_tait.md) tenure at [W+K Portland](../agencies/wieden_and_kennedy_portland.md) (2010–2012) and [W+K London](../agencies/wieden_and_kennedy_london.md) (2014–2021), and personally weighed in on Iain's appointment as a W+K Partner in December 2011. Iain wrote a personal tribute to him in Campaign after his death in 2022.

## Worked With Iain On

- **W+K Partnership** — As co-founder, Dan Wieden was the leadership figure who brought [Iain Tait](../collaborators/iain_tait.md) into [W+K Portland](../agencies/wieden_and_kennedy_portland.md) in 2010 and named him a Partner in 2011. *(evidence: raw/research/broad_iain_tait_2026-04-06.md — "W+K Partner Announcement...
