# Dan Wieden

Co-founder of Wieden+Kennedy and the agency's long-time creative leader. He led W+K during Iain Tait's tenure at W+K Portland (2010–2012) and W+K London (2014–2021), and personally weighed in on Iain's appointment as a W+K Partner in December 2011. Iain wrote a personal tribute to him in Campaign after his death in 2022.
