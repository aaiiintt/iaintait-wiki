# Helen Power

**Production Company:** Private Island

## Overview

Helen Power is the Co-Founder and Executive Producer of Private Island. Alongside Chris Boyle, she drives the company's production across live-action, intensive visual effects, and emerging generative AI tools.

## Key Projects

* **[Wearing Gillian (W+K)](../projects/wk_gillian_wearing_deepfake.md)** (2018)
    As Executive Producer at Private Island, Power oversaw the incredibly complex production required to execute this pioneering early deepfake art installation for artist Gillian Wearing.
