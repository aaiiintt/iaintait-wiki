# Marc Rouault

**Project:** Formula 1 Global Rebrand (2017)

## Overview

Marc Rouault is a typographer who designed the three custom typefaces at the heart of the Formula One global rebrand led by Wieden+Kennedy London. The three typefaces — **F1 Regular**, **F1 Turbo**, and **F1 Torque** — became the global typographic standard for all F1 communications following the 2017 rebrand.

## Key Projects

- **[Formula 1 Global Rebrand — Engineered Insanity](../projects/wk_f1_global_rebrand.md)** — Designed F1 Regular, F1 Turbo, and F1 Torque typefaces (2017). These remain the official typefaces for Formula One communications worldwide.
