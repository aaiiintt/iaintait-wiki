# Marc Rouault

French typographer and type designer. Under the creative direction of Richard Turley at Wieden+Kennedy London, he designed the three custom brand typefaces — F1 Regular, F1 Turbo, and F1 Torque — that serve as the typographic standard for Formula One communications.
