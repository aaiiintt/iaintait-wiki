# Nicky Gibson

## Overview

Nicky Gibson was a senior art director and designer at POKE London. She describes herself as "MoMA and Design Museum exhibited" — her personal site lists exhibitions at MoMA (New York), the Design Museum (London), and ICA (London). She has a confirmed MoMA artist page at `moma.org/artists/39782`. Her POKE client credits include Orange (Unlimited and Balloonacy).

## Key Projects

- **[Orange Unlimited](../projects/poke_orange_unlimited.md)** — Designer / Art Director, POKE London (2007). Listed in Campaign Annual 2007 creatives credits.
- **[Orange Balloonacy](../projects/poke_balloonacy.md)** — listed in her POKE client credits.
- **[Kate Moss for Topshop](../projects/poke_kate_moss_topshop.md)** — Senior Art Director / Designer, POKE London (2007). Both "Kate Moss" and "Topshop" listed as separate POKE-era clients on her portfolio.
