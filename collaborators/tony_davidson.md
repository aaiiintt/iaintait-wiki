# Tony Davidson

Executive Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy London for roughly two decades. Joined W+K in 2000 with then-creative partner Kim Papworth; became a global partner in 2009. After Papworth's departure in 2014, Iain Tait joined as his fellow ECD in London, and the pair led the London office together until Davidson stepped down in 2021. *(Source: Campaign, Creative Review career journey, W+K London partners announcement.)* In 2016 the British Arrows gave Davidson the Chairman's Award for his contribution to advertising. *(Source: More About Advertising.)*
