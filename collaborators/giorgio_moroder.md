# Giorgio Moroder

**Specialty:** Synthesizer orchestration, film scoring.

## Overview

Known as the "Father of Disco," Giorgio Moroder is an Italian composer and legendary music producer. His pioneering use of synthesizers in the 1970s heavily influenced the direction of electronic and pop music.

## Key Collaborations

* **[Racer (Chrome Experiment)](../projects/google_racer.md)** (Google Creative Lab)
    *In a highly unique collision of classic electronic music and modern browser technology, Moroder composed the original soundtrack for Google's multi-device mobile game experiment, Racer. The track dynamically synced across the devices. To bridge the digital experiment with tangible culture, the track was released as a physical 12-inch vinyl single and published officially on YouTube.*
