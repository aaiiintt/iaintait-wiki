# Freddy Taylor

Freddy Taylor is a creative and art director at W+K London with an idiosyncratic, often comic visual style. He graduated from the University of Edinburgh with a BA in Graphic Design, then worked at Netherlands-based KesselsKramer before joining W+K London in 2015. He works as a long-standing creative duo with **[Philippa Beaumont](philippa_beaumont.md)** — the pair have their own shared portfolio at [freddyandphilippa.com](https://www.freddyandphilippa.com).
