# Dan Norris

Creative director and one half of the long-running "Dan and Ray" creative team with [Ray Shaughnessy](ray_shaughnessy.md). Spent years at Wieden+Kennedy London as a Creative Director before the pair joined McCann London as ECDs in 2020, exited McCann, and were later promoted to Executive Creative Directors at Pablo London. *(sources: Campaign, LBBOnline)*
