# Nick Farnhill

Nick Farnhill co-founded POKE London in 2001 as one of six founders (Iain Tait, Nik Roope, Simon Waterfall, Peter Beech, Tom Hostler) and served as Chief Executive throughout the agency's independent years. He remained CEO through the Publicis Groupe acquisition in September 2013, continuing to run Publicis.Poke until later co-founding **FOOD** with Iain Tait and Richard Turley in 2021. *(evidence: raw/archive/food_creative_studio.md; raw/research/poke_london_agency_2026-04-06.md)*
