# Nick Farnhill

## Overview

Nick Farnhill co-founded POKE London alongside Iain Tait, Nik Roope, and Simon Waterfall. He served as Chief Executive Officer of the agency throughout its independent years. POKE was founded c.2000–2001 by alumni of Deepend and Oven Digital following the dotcom bust.
