# Susan Hoffman

## Overview

Susan Hoffman is a long-tenured Creative Director and Partner at Wieden+Kennedy Portland, credited on the Old Spice Responses campaign. She is one of W+K's most senior creative leaders.

A long-tenured Wieden+Kennedy Portland creative leader; she co-led W+K Portland as Executive Creative Director alongside Mark Fitzloff during Iain Tait's 2010–2012 tenure at the office.
