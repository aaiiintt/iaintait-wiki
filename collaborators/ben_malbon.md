# Ben Malbon

Co-founder of BBH Labs (2008) and Executive Director of Innovation at BBH New York before joining Google in 2010. At Google Creative Lab NYC he rose to Managing Director (2011–2013), a period during which The Guardian credits him as having "led work on Chrome, Chromebooks and Chrome experiments". Subsequently moved into Creative Partnerships and VP-level brand and creative roles; now VP Brand, Creative & Accelerating AI at Google, based in EMEA. Twitter: [@malbonnington](https://twitter.com/malbonnington). Non-executive director of Scottish Ballet.
