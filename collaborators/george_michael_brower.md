# George Michael Brower

Interaction designer and creative technologist. At Google Creative Lab 2010–2015, where he built Chrome Experiments and web-based music videos. Known for co-building *The Wilderness Downtown* with Arcade Fire — a browser-based music video that won the Cannes Grand Prix. After GCL: founded Always & Forever Computer Entertainment (2015–2020), then moved to Google's Tech & Society team designing tools for AI co-creation. Currently Staff UX Engineer at Google. Open-source author of lil-gui (1.5k GitHub stars) and gee.js. Handle: @georgealways.
