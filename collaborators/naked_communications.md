# Naked Communications

Communications planning and media strategy agency founded in 2000 in London by John Harlow, Jon Wilkins and Will Collin (all former executives at Omnicom's PHD). Known for channel-neutral, unconventional strategy, Naked was acquired by Australia's Photon Group in 2008. They were a partner on POKE London's Warholiser project for Tate Modern (2002).
