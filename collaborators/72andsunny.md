# 72andSunny

Global creative advertising agency founded in 2004 by John Boiler, Glenn Cole, Robert Nakata and Greg Perlot, with founding offices in Los Angeles and Amsterdam. Named Ad Age Agency of the Year 2012 and Adweek Agency of the Year 2013. In 2013 72andSunny was appointed as a lead agency on the Google Chrome business, partnering with Google Creative Lab during Iain Tait's tenure there.
