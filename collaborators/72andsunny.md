## Background & Forensics

Appointed as the global advertising agency for the Chrome business in 2013, 72andSunny partnered with Google Creative Lab to execute the massive product launch campaign for the Chromebook. They also drove the broader Google Chrome campaign, which included pop-culture insertions (like the Family Guy / Stewie spot).
