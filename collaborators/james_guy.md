# James Guy

Production lead who spent around 14–15 years at Wieden+Kennedy London, serving as Head of Integrated Production for his final five years at the agency before joining Uncommon Creative Studio as Chief Production Officer. Per the Uncommon announcement, he "was directly responsible for all output, leading some of the agency's most memorable campaigns including Nike Londoner as well as work for the likes of Honda, Three, Lurpak, and Coca Cola." *(source: Uncommon/Medium announcement; SHOOTonline)*
