# Andrew Zolty

## Overview

Andrew Zolty was a creative at POKE London. He is the creator of **Baker Tweet** — the Arduino-powered Twitter device built for The Albion café in Shoreditch (April 2009), directly across the road from POKE's office. The project was later exhibited at MoMA's "Talk to Me" show (2011).
