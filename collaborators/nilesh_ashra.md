# Nilesh Ashra

Creative technologist. Lead developer at POKE London in the late 2000s, joined Wieden+Kennedy Portland as creative technology lead in late 2011, founded W+K's emerging-tech incubator **The Lodge** in 2013 and ran it for five years, then co-founded the studio **Two Things** with Paulo Ribeiro in March 2018, and currently runs **OK Tomorrow** as a creativity-and-emerging-tech consultancy. Innovation Advisor to the LA Philharmonic.

## Career

- **POKE London** — Lead Developer, late 2000s. Worked on Orange Balloonacy, Spot the Bull, and other POKE-era projects under Iain Tait.
- **Wieden+Kennedy Portland** — Creative Technology Lead, joined late 2011. *(Adweek)*
- **The Lodge (W+K Portland)** — Founder & Director of Creative Technology, 2013–2018. Five years running W+K's emerging-tech incubator across AI, VR, robotics, and experience design. Notable projects credited in press: Nike Live Design custom sneaker lab, Samsung Gear VR intro experience, KFC VR training, Anki Cozmo Lost in Reddit, a functional Verizon network inside Minecraft. *(Adweek, The Drum)*
- **Two Things** — Co-founder with Paulo Ribeiro, March 2018. Portland-based digital creative agency focused on emerging tech. *(Fast Company, The Drum)*
- **OK Tomorrow** — Founder. Creativity-and-emerging-tech consultancy. *(LBB Online)*
- **LA Philharmonic** — Innovation Advisor. *(LBB Online)*
