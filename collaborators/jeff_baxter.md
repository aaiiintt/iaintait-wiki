# Jeff Baxter

## Overview

Jeff Baxter was an instrumental creative at Google Creative Lab, known for pushing the boundaries of what browser-based experiences could accomplish.

## Key Projects

* **[Racer (Google Creative Lab)](../projects/google_racer.md)** (2013)
    Baxter was a core part of the Google Creative Lab team collaborating on the highly successful Chrome Experiment Racer.
* **[Coder (Raspberry Pi)](../projects/google_coder.md)** (2013)
    Baxter was a key designer working on the Coder open-source education platform with Jason Striegel.
