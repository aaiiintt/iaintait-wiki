# Tom Hostler

Co-founder of POKE London (2001), where he served as Managing Director — internally nicknamed "Boss Hoss". He started his design career in the mid-90s building commercial websites for clients including Apple and BBC, came into POKE from the Deepend/DeepGroup lineage, and after POKE's acquisition by Publicis in 2013 focused on complex digital products and creative-tech experiences for brands such as EE. He is currently Chief Digital Officer at Koto, the design studio's first appointment to that role. *(sources: [Creative Review, Jan 2025](https://www.creativereview.co.uk/tom-hostler-koto-digital-design/); [The Brand Identity interview](https://the-brandidentity.com/interview/listen-twice-cut-once-repeat-kotos-cdo-tom-hostler-on-measuring-the-success-of-digital); [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hostler/); internal: `agencies/poke_london.md`, `raw/research/poke_london_agency_2026-04-06.md`)*
