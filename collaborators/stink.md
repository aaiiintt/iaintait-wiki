# Stink Films

Creatively-driven global production company established in 1998 (Stink Limited incorporated 24 September 1997) and founded by Daniel Bergmann, who serves as Founder and President. Registered at 64–66 Redchurch Street, London. Stink Films represents a global roster of directors and, alongside sister company Stink Studios, produces award-winning film, photography and craft across advertising, entertainment, music and the arts, with offices in London, Los Angeles, Berlin, Paris, Shanghai, São Paulo and beyond.
