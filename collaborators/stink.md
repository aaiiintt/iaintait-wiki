# Stink Films

**Role Focus:** Commercial Production
**Key Agencies:** Wieden+Kennedy London

## Associated Projects

* [Harry Potter: Wizards Unite Global Launch](../projects/wk_harry_potter_wizards_unite.md) (2019)

## Background & Forensics

Stink Films is a global creative production company. They co-produced the launch film for Niantic's *Harry Potter: Wizards Unite* alongside W+K London and The Mill (directed by Tom Green), treating the mobile game launch with the production value and scale of a theatrical release.
