**Role Focus:** VFX, Post-Production, Creative Execution
**Key Agencies:** Wieden+Kennedy London

## Associated Projects

* [Dream Makers](../projects/wk_honda_dream_makers.md) (2017)

## Background & Forensics

Time Based Arts is an independent creative studio in London specializing in visual effects and post-production. They collaborated closely with W+K London on Honda's "Dream Makers," helping executing the technically complex, multi-genre visual deconstruction of the filmmaking process.
