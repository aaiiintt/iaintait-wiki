# Time Based Arts

Independent London-based VFX, post-production and colour studio. Frequent W+K London post partner across Iain's ECD tenure, credited on multiple Creative Circle and D&AD-winning films.

## Worked With Iain On

- **[Honda: Dream Makers](../projects/wk_honda_dream_makers.md)** — Director / production / post in full on the Channel 4 idents campaign (W+K London, 2017). *(evidence: projects/wk_honda_dream_makers.md)*
- **[Nike: Nothing Beats a Londoner](../projects/wk_nothing_beats_a_londoner.md)** — Post / VFX, with Sheldon Gardner and Francois Roisin as VFX Supervisors and Simone Grattarola on colour (W+K London, 2018). *(evidence: projects/wk_nothing_beats_a_londoner.md)*
- **[Three: Phones Are Good](../projects/wk_three_phones_are_good.md)** — VFX and grade, winning Creative Circle Best VFX/CGI and Best Grade (W+K London). *(evidence: projects/wk_three_phones_are_good.md)*
- **[Sainsbury's: Nicholas the Sweep](../projects/wk_sainsburys_nicholas_the_sweep.md)** — VFX, with Leo Weston and Sam Osborne as VFX Supervisors and Lewis Crossfield on colour (W+K London, 2019). *(evidence: projects/wk_sainsburys_nicholas_the_sweep.md)*
- **[Facebook: Still Going Strong](../projects/wk_facebook_still_going_strong.md)** — VFX, with Luke Todd as VFX Lead and Lewis Crossfield on colour (W+K London). *(evidence: projects/wk_facebook_still_going_strong.md)*
- **[National Trust: Everyone Needs Nature](../projects/wk_national_trust_everyone_needs_nature.md)** — Post production (W+K London). *(evidence: projects/wk_national_trust_everyone_needs_nature.md)*
