# Dom Felton

## Overview

Dom Felton has been at Wieden+Kennedy London since November 2010, progressing from Producer to Senior Producer to Executive Producer. He produced the Coca-Cola / Maroon 5 "24hr Recording Session" (2011) and was the producer across all of W+K London's Niantic campaigns.

Prior to W+K: Ogilvy Interactive (Interactive Producer, Nokia projects), Holler (Account Manager/Producer). Background in multimedia from Macquarie University, Sydney.
