# Peter Beech

Peter Beech is a co-founder of POKE London (2001), where he joined Iain Tait, Nicolas Roope, Nick Farnhill, Simon Waterfall and Tom Hostler in the basement of Mother London after the collapse of their previous dotcom-era agencies. Prior to POKE, Beech had been managing director of Deepend London, having joined from Webmedia and Leo Burnett ([Campaign, Deepend hires Peter Beech as new managing director](https://www.campaignlive.co.uk/article/deepend-hires-peter-beech-new-managing-director/41648); [Lindberg interview with Simon Waterfall](https://medium.com/the-lindberg-interviews/interview-with-creative-director-simon-waterfall-e4da661ad08e)). He remained one of the four founding shareholders of POKE through the 2013 Publicis acquisition, after Tait and Waterfall had departed (*evidence: raw/research/poke_london_agency_2026-04-06.md*).
