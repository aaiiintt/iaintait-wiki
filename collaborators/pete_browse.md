# Pete Browse

**Creative, Wieden+Kennedy London** (later Creative Director, adam&eveDDB)

Computer Science graduate who combined scriptwriting for TV campaigns with digital production expertise. Also co-founded a creative collective working on interactive music and fashion projects (collaborated with Sigur Rós).

Jury member, One Club Young Ones 2017 Portfolio — Game Development category.

Later moved from W+K London to **adam&eveDDB** (Campaign Live: "Adam & Eve/DDB poaches second Wieden & Kennedy creative director in a year").
