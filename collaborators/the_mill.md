# The Mill

Global VFX, colour and post-production studio (founded London, 1990). Long-standing W+K London post partner on large-scale commercial work.

## Worked With Iain On

- **[Three: Go Roam / Holidays](../projects/wk_three_go_roam.md)** — VFX, with Alex Hammond as VFX Supervisor (W+K London, 2017). *(evidence: projects/wk_three_go_roam.md)*
- **[Coca-Cola: The Letter](../projects/wk_coca_cola_the_letter.md)** — VFX (London), with Barnsley as VFX Supervisor and Sid Harrington-Odedra as Lead Artist. *(evidence: projects/wk_coca_cola_the_letter.md)*
- **[Three: Real 5G](../projects/wk_three_real_5g.md)** — VFX on the 5G launch campaign (W+K London). *(evidence: projects/wk_three_real_5g.md)*
- **[Harry Potter: Wizards Unite](../projects/wk_harry_potter_wizards_unite.md)** — VFX / post on the global launch film (W+K London, 2019), with Stefan Susemihi and Dan Moller leading VFX and James Bamford on colour. *(evidence: projects/wk_harry_potter_wizards_unite.md)*
- **[Rovio: Angry Birds 2](../projects/wk_rovio_angry_birds_2.md)** — VFX, with Andrew "Barnsley" Wood on 2D and Adam Droy on 3D (W+K London). *(evidence: projects/wk_rovio_angry_birds_2.md)*
