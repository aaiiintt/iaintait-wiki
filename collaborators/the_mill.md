# The Mill

**Role Focus:** VFX, Post-Production
**Key Agencies:** Wieden+Kennedy London

## Associated Projects

* [Harry Potter: Wizards Unite Global Launch](../projects/wk_harry_potter_wizards_unite.md) (2019)

## Background & Forensics

The Mill is a legacy, multi-award-winning global production and VFX studio. They worked with W+K London on the major cinematic launch film for Niantic's *Harry Potter: Wizards Unite*, creating the visual effects that brought Dementors and wizardry seamlessly into a real-world setting.
