# Jackie Jantos

## Overview

Jackie Jantos was the client contact on the Coca-Cola / Maroon 5 "24hr Recording Session" at W+K Portland (2011), per Iain Tait. *(Note: Joe Belliotti, Director of Global Entertainment Marketing, is the Coca-Cola name that appears in contemporaneous press — Jantos's specific title and role on this project is confirmed by Iain Tait but not independently verified in open sources.)*

She went on to become one of the most prominent brand and marketing executives of her generation.
