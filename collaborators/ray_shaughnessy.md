# Ray Shaughnessy

Creative director who spent roughly thirteen years at Wieden+Kennedy London (joined 2006) as one half of a long-standing creative team with Dan Norris, working on accounts including Three, Formula 1, Lurpak, Sainsbury's, Tesco, Bud Light and Fairtrade. Shaughnessy and Norris left W+K London to join McCann London as Executive Creative Directors in 2020, and subsequently exited McCann. *(sources: Campaign, LBBonline, Adweek)*
