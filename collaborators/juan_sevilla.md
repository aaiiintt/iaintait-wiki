# Juan Sevilla

Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy London; per LinkedIn and The Org, has been at W+K since February 2016, working across brands including Honda, Coca-Cola, Heinz, National Trust and the ATP Tour. *(Source: LinkedIn, The Org, The Drum.)* Credited on W+K London's "It All Adds Up" ATP Tour platform (2025) and Heinz masterbrand work with Joe De Souza. *(Source: The Drum.)*
