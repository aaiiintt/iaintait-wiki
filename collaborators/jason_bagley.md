# Jason Bagley

## Overview

Jason Bagley is a Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy Portland who was one of the core creative team behind the Old Spice Response campaign — the 2010 real-time social media project that produced 186 personalised video responses in 2.5 days and became the defining proof-of-concept for real-time branded content.

He held a multi-role credit on the project: Creative Director, Art Director, Director, and Writer.
