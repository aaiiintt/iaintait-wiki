# Niantic

AR and location-based gaming company founded April 20, 2010 as an internal Google incubator by John Hanke (co-founder of Keyhole, the geospatial startup that became Google Earth). Niantic spun out of Google in October 2015 with a $30M investment from Google, Nintendo and The Pokémon Company, and launched Pokémon GO globally in July 2016. The company is also behind Ingress and Harry Potter: Wizards Unite. W+K London partnered with Niantic on multiple launch campaigns 2017–2019 during Iain Tait's ECD tenure, and FOOD later collaborated with them on Superblue: JR Reality.
