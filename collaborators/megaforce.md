# Megaforce

Paris-based directing collective comprising Leo Berne, Clément Gallet, Charles Brisgand and Raphaël Rodriguez — four French directors from backgrounds in graphic arts, design, photography and advertising, brought to Riff Raff Films in 2010. Known for kinetic, visually inventive commercial and music video work.

## Worked With Iain On

- **[Nike: Nothing Beats a Londoner](../projects/wk_nothing_beats_a_londoner.md)** — Directors (W+K London × Nike, 2018). Shot on 16mm across London's boroughs in a "passing the baton" cascade; won the Cannes Film Grand Prix. *(evidence: projects/wk_nothing_beats_a_londoner.md)*
