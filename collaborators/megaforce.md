# Megaforce

**Type:** Production

## Overview

Megaforce is an acclaimed French directing collective known for their highly inventive, visually dynamic, and kinetic commercial and music video work.

They most famously collaborated with Wieden+Kennedy London on the iconic, Cannes Grand Prix-winning Nike campaign ["Nothing Beats a Londoner"](../projects/wk_nike_nothing_beats_a_londoner.md) (2018), which showcased their signature fast-paced, seamless transition style.
