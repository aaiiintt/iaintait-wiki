# Derek Man Lui

Creative originally from San Francisco, now an Executive Creative Director at Mother, promoted into the role alongside Tomas Coleman and reporting to global CCO Felix Richter. Prior stops per Campaign US include Creative Director at Droga5 New York, Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy (London), and earlier roles across AKQA, BBH Group, Proximity Worldwide and Duncan/Channon in the US and China. He maintains a personal portfolio at derekmanlui.com and runs a joint studio site with Tomas Coleman at derekandtomas.com. *(sources: [Campaign US](https://www.campaignlive.com/article/mother-promotes-tomas-coleman-derek-man-lui-ecds/1943187), [derekmanlui.com](https://derekmanlui.com), [derekandtomas.com](https://derekandtomas.com), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dereklui/))*
