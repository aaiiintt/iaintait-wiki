# Joel Gethin Lewis

Interaction designer and creative technologist. Co-founded **Hellicar & Lewis** (2008–2016) with Pete Hellicar, an open-source craft/design/technology studio, and was a co-founder of **YesYesNo** (2008) with Theo Watson and Zach Lieberman. Previously interaction designer at United Visual Artists (touring with U2 and Massive Attack). Currently Interactive Creative Director at Universal Everything and Course Leader of the UAL Diploma in Apple Development at the Creative Computing Institute, London. *(sources: [joelgethinlewis.com](https://joelgethinlewis.com/), [CreativeApplications.Net](https://www.creativeapplications.net/people/joel-gethin-lewis/), [Universal Everything](https://www.universaleverything.com/about-us/joel-gethin-lewis))*
