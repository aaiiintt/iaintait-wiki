# Marius Watz

## Overview

Marius Watz is a Norwegian artist and programmer known for generative visual art and code-based work (previously at unlekker.net). He was directly named by Nik Roope in the Dezeen launch coverage for Orange Unlimited (November 2007): *"We worked with... an art coder guy called Mariuz Watz."* He contributed the generative / algorithmic visual elements of the never-ending webpage.

## Key Projects

- **[Orange Unlimited](../projects/poke_orange_unlimited.md)** — Art Coder / Generative Artist (2007). Named by Nik Roope in Dezeen; listed in Campaign Annual 2007 creatives.
