# Duane King

**Era:** W+K Portland (c.2010–2011); King/Coyle (2010–2015)

## Overview

Duane King is an interaction designer who co-led the design and interaction work on *Nike Better World* alongside Ian Coyle — producing the parallax scrolling effect that made the site the most influential web design of 2011.

Following the project, Duane King co-founded **King/Coyle** with Ian Coyle, a studio that continued developing the interaction language they pioneered at W+K. His portfolio at kingduane.com confirmed the FWA Site of the Day and Cannes Cyber Shortlist credits for Nike Better World.

## Key Projects

- **[Nike Better World](../projects/wk_nike_better_world.md)** — Design and Interaction co-lead (2011). Co-created the parallax scrolling effect alongside Ian Coyle.
