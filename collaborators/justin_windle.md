# Justin Windle

Creative technologist and engineer, known by the handle **soulwire**. At Google Creative Lab 2012–2015 as Creative Technologist, contributing to Chrome Experiments including Photowall for Chromecast. Prior: Lean Mean Fighting Machine (2009–2011), Fantasy Interactive (2011–2012). Post-GCL: Spotify (Senior Software Engineer, 2017–2021), now CTO at TORTUS AI. Prominent open-source author — sketch.js (4.1k+ GitHub stars), Makisu, Coffee-Physics. Speaks at Resonate and FITC conferences. Based in London.
