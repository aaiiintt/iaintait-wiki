# Ian Pons Jewell

## Overview

Ian Pons Jewell is a commercials director best known for large-scale, visually ambitious films. He directed *Phones Are Good* for Three and W+K London (2018) and its sequel *Real 5G* (2020) — both epic-scale comedy films with significant VFX production. He is represented through Friend London.

## Key Projects

- **[Three: Phones Are Good](../projects/wk_three_phones_are_good.md)** — Director (2018). 2:33 historical comedy film for Three and W+K London. One Show winner; ~£189M incremental revenue uplift.
- **Three: Real 5G** (2020) — Director. Direct sequel campaign, also produced with W+K London.
