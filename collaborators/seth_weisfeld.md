# Seth Weisfeld

**Era:** W+K Portland (c.2010–2012)

## Overview

Seth Weisfeld was the Interactive Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy Portland who led the direct production of *Nike Better World* — the 2011 website universally credited as the first mainstream parallax scrolling website. He worked under the executive oversight of Iain Tait (Global Interactive ECD).

## Key Projects

- **[Nike Better World](../projects/wk_nike_better_world.md)** — Interactive Creative Director (2011). Led the build of the HTML5 infinite-scroll parallax website with Ian Coyle and Duane King, without any framework, starting from reset styles alone.
