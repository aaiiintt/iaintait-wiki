# Joris Philippart

## Overview

Joris Philippart (Belgian/Dutch name, sometimes phonetically recalled as "Yoris") was an art director at W+K London, working as part of the "Jason and Joris" creative pairing with copywriter Jason Scott under ECDs Iain Tait and Tony Davidson.
