# Jonathan Plackett

Creative technologist and senior creative at Wieden+Kennedy London. Prior to W+K he founded I Think And Do (maker of the Face Juggler app) and worked at BETC London and Albion London. He is also co-author, with Karrie Fransman, of *Gender Swapped Fairy Tales* and *Gender Swapped Greek Myths*. *(sources: [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jonathanplackett/), [It's Nice That](https://www.itsnicethat.com/articles/terms-and-conditions-apply-game-jonathan-plackett-alex-bellos-digital-150721))*
