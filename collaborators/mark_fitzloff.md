# Mark Fitzloff

## Overview

Mark Fitzloff was a Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy Portland credited on the Old Spice Responses campaign. He was named a W+K Partner in December 2011 — the same announcement that made Iain Tait a Partner.

## Key Projects

- **[Old Spice: Responses](../projects/wk_old_spice_responses.md)** — Creative Director (2010). *(evidence: projects/wk_old_spice_responses.md)*
- **[Coca-Cola Polar Bowl](../projects/wk_coca_cola_polar_bowl.md)** — Executive Creative Director, W+K Portland (Super Bowl XLVI, Feb 2012). *(evidence: projects/wk_coca_cola_polar_bowl.md)*
- **[Dodge Tent Event](../projects/wk_dodge_tent_event.md)** — Co-ECD, W+K Portland. *(evidence: projects/wk_dodge_tent_event.md)*
