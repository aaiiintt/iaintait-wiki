# Igor Clark

Technical director and creative technologist. At POKE London (2005–2008) as Technical Director, then Wieden+Kennedy Portland (2010–2012) as Creative Technology Director, then Google Creative Lab NYC (2013–2015) as Creative Tech Lead. Now freelance and London-based, also Tech Director at ArtRabbit. Specialises in scalable, interactive software — installations, AR, 5G edge caching, large-scale web systems. Remains hands-on with code, with a fondness for Erlang/OTP.
