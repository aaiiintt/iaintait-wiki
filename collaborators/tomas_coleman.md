# Tomas Coleman

Creative from Pembrokeshire, Wales; started his career at The Do Lectures and Hiut Denim before joining Wieden+Kennedy London, where he formed a long-running creative partnership with Derek Man Lui. The pair later moved to Droga5 New York and joined Mother London in 2024, where they were promoted to Executive Creative Directors (per LBB Online and Campaign, covering their promotion announcement). Tomas and Derek publish joint work at derekandtomas.com.
