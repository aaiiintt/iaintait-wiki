# Hollie Walker

## Overview

Hollie Walker served as Creative Director on *Phones Are Good* for Three at W+K London (2018), working between ECDs Iain Tait and Tony Davidson and the creative team of Tom Bender (AD) and Tom Corcoran (CW).

## Key Projects

- **[Three: Phones Are Good](../projects/wk_three_phones_are_good.md)** — Creative Director (2018). The campaign delivered ~£189M incremental revenue uplift and won a One Show 2019 award.
- **[Three: Real 5G](../projects/wk_three_real_5g.md)** — Creative Director, W+K London. *(evidence: projects/wk_three_real_5g.md)*
- **[TK Maxx: Ridiculous Possibilities](../projects/wk_tk_maxx_ridiculous_possibilities.md)** — Creative Director (long-standing duo with Freddie Powell; met at Central Saint Martins). *(evidence: projects/wk_tk_maxx_ridiculous_possibilities.md; [Behance](https://www.behance.net/freddieandhollie))*
