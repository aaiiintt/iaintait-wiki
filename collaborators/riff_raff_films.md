# Riff Raff Films

London-based, multi-award-winning production company founded by Matthew Fone, who started Riff Raff in 2010 (incorporated February 2009). An ideas-led shop, Riff Raff hosts a hand-picked roster of around 11 directors including Megaforce, François Rousselet, The Sacred Egg, David Wilson and Finn Keenan. The company has won Gold and Silver Cannes Lions, British Arrows, D&AD Yellow Pencils and Shots awards. Matthew Fone was named Creative Circle Production Company of the Year in 2017 and 2018 and Shots Production Company of the Year 2018; Riff Raff expanded to the US in 2019.
