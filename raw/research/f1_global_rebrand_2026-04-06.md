## Dimension 6 — Cultural Legacy

- First time F1 ever engaged a global creative agency — previous marketing was in-house/ad hoc
- Ended 23 years of brand stasis (Carter Wong 1994 identity)
- Liberty Media acquisition context: part of transformation from closed motorsport property to open global entertainment brand
- Preceded Drive to Survive (Netflix, 2019) which drove F1's massive audience growth
- Flamingo Research: 6-month global brand health study informed the brief
- Cited in W+K London's 20-year retrospective as a milestone work
