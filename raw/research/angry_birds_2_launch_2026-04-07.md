## 3. Creative Concept

**Campaign tagline/concept:** "Bigger. Badder. Birdier."

**Hero film:** 60-second live-action/VFX online film
- Setting: "Piggy Island" — a luxurious tropical paradise where smug pigs frolic
- Narrative: Birds plan revenge on the pigs who stole their eggs; attack the island
- Tone: Over-the-top action/comedy. "Completely mad porcine action/comedy" (Stash)
- Production: Shot on location at Big Major Cay, Bahamas (real island where wild pigs swim in the sea) — the physical reality of "swimming pigs" was a key casting/location decision

**Multi-channel social programme:**
- Pre-launch: @smugpigsofinstagram Instagram account — parodied "Rich Kids of Instagram", showed pigs living the good life on Piggy Island to build anticipation and envy
- Propaganda-style posters counting down to launch on Angry Birds social channels
- Bespoke illustrated poster commissioned from artist Paul Shipper
- Post-launch: Birds vs Pigs Spotify playlists, Angry Birds Tumblr re-skin, 'Wanted Pigs' posters, 'Got Eggs?' social posts

**Global scope:** The campaign launched globally on 30 July 2015 (game available worldwide on iOS and Android). Campaign Live UK noted it as "UK campaign" but LBB framing and Rovio's global game release context indicate worldwide digital distribution.

**W+K relationship with Rovio:** This was explicitly described as "Wieden+Kennedy's first work for Rovio" (Campaign US, August 2015).

## 6. Metrics

### 50 Million Downloads
- **Primary confirmation:** MediaPost (26 Aug 2015): "Angry Birds 2 had 50 million downloads in its first [weeks]"
  - URL: https://www.mediapost.com/publications/omma-today/edition/2015/08/26/
- **Guardian confirmation:** Article headline "Despite 50m downloads of Angry Birds 2 game, 38% headcount reduction"
  - URL: https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2015/aug/26/angry-birds-rovio-lay-off-260-staff
  - Date: 26 August 2015 — i.e., 50M downloads achieved by ~late August 2015, approximately 4 weeks after 30 July launch
- **Medium / secondary source:** "The game reached 50 million downloads in just 35 days"
  - URL: https://shahmm.medium.com/from-slingshot-to-downfall-how-angry-birds-revolutionized-mobile-gaming-and-lost-its-flight-bb3124b9d087
  - Note: This appears to apply to an earlier Angry Birds title milestone, not specifically AB2. Context in source ambiguous.

### Other game performance data (Rovio context from PocketGamer.biz)
- URL: https://www.pocketgamer.biz/how-rovios-licensing-and-games-business-have-evolved-over-the-last-decade/
- Date: 29 May 2019
- Rovio marketing director Stephen Porter at Casual Connect Europe noted 2015 was "a pretty dark year for Rovio" operationally (headcount cut from 690 to 470) despite total portfolio downloads reaching 3.1 billion lifetime
- However: "Angry Birds 2... got off to a poor start [operationally], but successful live ops have seen the game change significantly and become the company's flagship game. In 2018 it brought in €117m in revenue on its own, bringing the game's lifetime sales to €253m."
- This provides important nuance: the launch succeeded in downloads but the game was early-stage; long-term it became Rovio's most valuable title

### Context on "50 million"
- The original Angry Birds game reached 50M downloads as a different milestone (2010 era)
- Angry Birds 2 specifically: 50M downloads confirmed in first ~4 weeks (July 30 – late August 2015)
- The Guardian headline (26 Aug 2015) is the clearest contemporary confirmation linking "50M downloads" to Angry Birds 2 specifically
