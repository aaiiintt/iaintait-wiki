## Cannes Lions — Cyber Lions Jury President (2012)

- **Role:** Jury President (as Global Interactive Executive Creative Director, Wieden+Kennedy; by ceremony time, ECD Google Creative Lab)
- **Year:** 2012 (59th Cannes Lions, June 17–23, 2012)
- **Announced:** February 3, 2012

**Sources:**
- [PRNewswire (March 8, 2012)](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/usa-today-announces-us-jurors-for-2012-cannes-lions-international-festival-of-creativity-141901333.html)
- [LBBOnline (April 5, 2012)](https://lbbonline.com/news/cannes-lions-announces-three-further-juries)
- [AdHugger announcement (Feb 3, 2012)](https://www.adhugger.net/2012/02/03/david-jones-iain-tait-and-mainardo-de-nardis-to-head-juries-at-cannes-lions-2012/)
- [AdHugger full jury (Apr 4, 2012)](https://www.adhugger.net/2012/04/04/cannes-lions-announced-the-juries-for-cyber-radio-and-promo-lions-2012/)
- [BestMediaInfo (Feb 3, 2012)](https://bestmediainfo.com/2012/02/david-jones-to-chair-cannes-creative-effectiveness-jury)
- [ACN Newswire (Cannes official press release)](https://www.acnnewswire.com/press-release/english/9060/cannes-lions-announces-three-further-juries---cyber,-radio-and-promo-&-activation)
- [Bizcommunity](https://www.bizcommunity.com/Article/75/40/70259.html)
- **Tag:** `award`

**Iain Tait quote on appointment (February 2012):**
> "I'm excited to guide a Cyber Jury that brings together the best talent from around the globe to recognise the greatest work in the world. There's so much change taking place so rapidly in our space that it's going to be tough to make sense of it all. But I'm confident we'll prove this category is where creativity is vibrant, thriving, and shaping the industry."

**Iain Tait Grand Prix announcement speech (June 20, 2012):**
On Curators of Sweden Grand Prix:
> "Very simple, very smart and very brave... that they handed their voice over to the people says so much about who they are."

On both Grand Prix winners (Nike+ FuelBand and Curators of Sweden):
> "Both pieces are about brands behaving a certain way. They're not about what they say and noisy messages, they're about what brands do and that's what we're excited by."
> "We just felt these two pieces represented the kind of work that people should be creating."

**Source for speech:** [Fast Company (June 20, 2012)](https://www.fastcompany.com/1680967/nike-fuelband-and-curators-of-sweden-take-top-cyber-lions-at-cannes)

**Note:** Tait was appointed while at W+K, joined Google Creative Lab in May 2012, then presided at Cannes in June 2012 as Google's first major awards appearance.

## Andy Awards — Jury Member (2011, 2012, 2014)

- **Years:** 2011 (W+K), 2012 (W+K), 2014 (Google Creative Lab)
- **Source:** [ANDY Awards official jury history](https://www.andyawards.com/jury/)
- [andys.awardcore.com 2014](https://andys.awardcore.com/andys/about) — "Iain Tait, Google Creative Lab, New York"

**Iain Tait judging diary (2012 ANDY Awards, Kauai Hawaii):**
> "As an advertising award, the ANDYs are pretty unique. It's one set of judges that presides over work from the entire industry."
> "The most fundamental of all the conversations... is around what constitutes an idea."
> "I also have issues with the ad industry patting itself on the back for making digital products and services when we're only comparing ourselves to a limited set of the work that's out there."

**Source:** [Adweek AgencySpy (February 29, 2012)](https://www.adweek.com/agencyspy/iain-tait-gives-us-an-exclusive-look-into-andys-judging-in-hawaii/)
