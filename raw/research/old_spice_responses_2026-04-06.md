## Dimension 5 — Iain's Role

- Title: Global Interactive Executive Creative Director, W+K Portland
- Joined W+K April 2010; campaign launched July 2010
- Credited as Creative Director on all 3 One Show Gold Pencil entries (confirmed via official One Club records)
- Made W+K Partner in 2011 "in recognition of his contribution"
- Quote: "his most recognized work is the Old Spice 'Responses' campaign" — One Club Board bio: https://www.oneclub.org/directors/-bio/ian-tait/
- Fast Company interview July 14, 2010: https://www.fastcompany.com/1670314/team-who-made-old-spice-smell-good-again-reveals-whats-behind-mustafas-towel/

## Dimension 6 — Cultural Legacy

- Real-time social marketing model — widely cited as the pioneering example; inspired a "go-to practice for marketers" (D&AD)
- Ad Age Top 15 Campaigns of the 21st Century — #4
- Day 1 views outpaced Obama victory speech and Susan Boyle YouTube records (Visible Measures, via Mediapost)
- Fast Company (2020) 10th anniversary: "firmly cemented the brand's iconic pop-culture status"
- W+K 30th anniversary Webby feature: "Old Spice's 'The Man Your Man Could Smell Like' launched at the Super Bowl and then exploded across the web, with rapid-response videos that made social interaction the campaign itself"
- Source: https://www.webbyawards.com/webby30/most-iconic-companies-wiedenkennedy/
