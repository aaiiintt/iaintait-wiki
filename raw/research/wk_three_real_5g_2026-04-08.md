## Gaps & open questions

- **Editor Ben Campbell** — confirmed at D&AD but edit company not listed (on *Phones Are Good* the editor was also Ben Campbell at Cut & Run — same likely applies here but unconfirmed).
- **Sound: String & Tins** — confirmed at D&AD. The existing project file does not list the sound house — this is a new credit.
- **nineteentwenty and Frender** — confirmed as additional post-production houses at D&AD alongside The Mill. The existing project file lists only The Mill for VFX — these are additions.
- **Shaun Williamson** — search results mention Shaun Williamson (Barry from EastEnders) alongside Lewis Capaldi as talent in the spot. The existing project file credits only Lewis Capaldi. Shaun Williamson should be verified and added.
- **Production company discrepancy** — *Phones Are Good* used Friend London; *Real 5G* used Academy Films. Both credited Ian Pons Jewell as director. This is notable and confirmed across multiple sources.
- **One Show / Cannes Lions** — D&AD Yellow Pencil confirmed. CICLOPE Grand Prix confirmed. No Cannes Lion or One Show win found in research — may not have been entered or won at those shows.

## Summary

- **Well-evidenced:** Ian Pons Jewell directed both *Phones Are Good* (2018, Friend London) and *Real 5G* (2020, Academy Films) — same director, different production companies. Full D&AD 2021 credits confirmed (Yellow Pencil for Direction + Graphite Pencil for Production Design). CICLOPE Grand Prix confirmed. VFX by The Mill, nineteentwenty, Frender (three houses). Sound by String & Tins. Premiered 27 February / aired 29 February 2020 during Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway. Partner brands: Tinder, Samsung, YouTube Music, Deliveroo, Greggs (project file also notes KFC). Lewis Capaldi hologram confirmed.
- **Thin:** Shaun Williamson talent credit (mentioned in some sources but not confirmed from primary credits listing). Edit house (likely Cut & Run given Ben Campbell edited both Phones Are Good and Real 5G, but not confirmed).
- **Not found:** Cannes Lions win. One Show win. Full music credit.
