## Internal evidence (grep sweep)

- `projects/wk_national_trust_everyone_needs_nature.md` — Six TVCs across ITV, Channel 4, and Sky for six weeks from September 2020. COVID lockdown context: National Trust furloughed 80% of staff, severe financial losses. Campaign focus: direct donations to conservation targets (plant 20 million trees, create green corridors). Director / DOP: Anthony Dickenson (All Mighty Pictures). Post: Time Based Arts. Editor: Jack Williams (The Assembly Rooms). Sound: String and Tins. Media agency: Medialabs Group. ECDs: Iain Tait, Tony Davidson. CD: Flo Heiss. Creative: Derek Man Lui, Harry Ingrams, Joanna Cassidy Osborne, Joseph Paul, Richard Biggs. EP: James Guy. Also produced by Rose Fairley and Sahar Bluck.
- `raw/research/wk_london_brands_2026-04-07.md` — Launch date: 7 September 2020. Full credits confirmed. YouGov poll data (June 2020): 38% of adults said spending time in nature was what they looked forward to most during lockdown; 33% said interest in nature increased since lockdown; 68% agreed noticing nature made them feel happy during lockdown. Donation structure: £5 plants a tree, £10 maintains coastal footpaths, £15 helps wildflower meadow creation. Originally planned and paid for in March 2020 but put on hold during lockdown. Related earlier campaign: National Trust 125th Anniversary (January 2020). Director Anthony Dickenson's music video background: The XX, Jon Hopkins, Groove Armada. Production co All Mighty Pictures specialises in natural world films (WWF, World Animal Protection). Client: Mark Scott, Rebecca Cogswell, Duncan Ogle-Skan; Marketing Director: Shaherazad Umbreen.

## Campaign context
- National Trust furloughed 80% of staff during COVID-19 first wave
- Conservation work and projects paused / cancelled due to financial losses
- Campaign originally planned and paid for March 2020 — delayed by lockdown
- Launch timed to National Trust's 125th anniversary conservation ambitions
- Campaign ran six weeks from 7 September 2020 across ITV, Channel 4, Sky; billboards; national newspapers and magazines
