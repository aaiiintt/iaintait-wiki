## THREE / 3 UK CAMPAIGNS (W+K London)

### 8. Three – #HolidaySpam / Feel At Home Extension
- **Year:** January 2015
- **What:** Campaign preparing public for more holiday spam from Three's Feel At Home customers. Extension of 2014 apology campaign. Features Byron Bay surf trip + Grand Canyon holiday spammers. Voiced by Michael Burke.
- **Media:** 60", 30", 10" TV; cinema, VOD, press, social, radio, OOH, DOOH
- **URL (LBB):** https://lbbonline.com/news/public-prepped-for-more-holidayspam-with-new-wk-london-three-campaign

### 9. Three – #MakeItRight / Jackson the Muppet
- **Year:** June 2015
- **What:** Brand platform campaign. Jackson, a purple furry puppet by Jim Henson's Creature Shop. Song: East 17's "It's Alright" (1993). Directed by Daniel Wolfe through Somesuch.
- **Media partner:** Mindshare. OOH, outdoor Wi-Fi stations, interactive bus shelters with phone charging.
- **URL (LBB):** https://lbbonline.com/news/wk-london-threes-furry-friend-rocks-an-anthem-against-suckishness

### 10. Three – Go Roam / "Giraffe-amingo"
- **Year:** May 2017
- **What:** CGI ad — shy giraffe transforms into giraffe-flamingo hybrid to demonstrate freedom of Three's Go Roam network feature. First in planned series of animal hybrids.
- **Creatives:** Katy Edelsten, Chloe Cordon (W+K London)
- **CDs:** Larry Seftel, David Day
- **ECDs:** Tony Davidson, Iain Tait
- **Director:** The Perlorian Brothers (MJZ)
- **VFX:** The Mill
- **Account:** Paulo Salomao (GAD), Lex Higlett, Sophy Woltman
- **Channels:** TV, OOH, DOOH, Online, Cinema, Social, Print
- **URL (Campaign):** https://www.campaignlive.com/article/three-go-roam-wieden-kennedy-london/1434379
