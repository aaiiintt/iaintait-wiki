## Internal evidence (grep sweep)

- `projects/wk_brand_establishments.md` — Two major UK brand establishment campaigns in 2017. (1) Bud Light UK launch: OOH phase March 2017 ("Keep It Bud Light"), TV phase October 2017 directed by Ali Ali (Sonny London). (2) Tyrrells "Absurdly Good": first-ever TV campaign, £2.5m budget, launched 11 September 2017. Peter Serafinowicz voiceover. Dog & Rabbit production company. ECDs on both: Tony Davidson, Iain Tait. CDs on both: Dan Norris, Ray Shaughnessy.
- `raw/research/wk_london_brands_2026-04-07.md` — Full credits for both campaigns confirmed. Bud Light OOH: 13 March 2017 launch; AB InBev's biggest-ever UK brand campaign; multi-channel strategy reaching 90% of UK adults 14+ times; London IMAX, Birmingham Media Eyes. TV spot: October 2017, director Ali Ali described as "one of the world's ten most awarded directors per Gunn Report," first-ever UK ad. Production co: Sonny London. Tyrrells: two executions ("Very Important Potatoes" 20" TVC + "Fabulously Curly" 20" social film); tagline "It's absurd what we do before Tyrrells gets to you"; brand penetration had grown 35% in two years prior; ran until end of November 2017.
