## Dimension 6 — Cultural Legacy

- Mayor of London Sadiq Khan shared organically
- Drake commented he "couldn't believe he wasn't invited" — became viral itself
- The Guardian created a parody cartoon the next day
- Spawned "Nothing Beats a Brummy/Manc" etc. parody genre
- Royal Yachting Association made official parody
- UM survey: 56% of non-Londoners feel ads don't represent them — NBAL became the central exhibit in the "London bubble" debate
- Creative Review 2023: "Ok advertising, it's time to let go of Nothing Beats a Londoner" — confirms its defining status
- LBB Immortal Awards: "If Nike's 'Good vs Evil' set the bar for advertising in the '90s, this piece encapsulates millennial culture"
- Nike replicated format for Shanghai ("Shanghai's Never Done", March 2019)
