## Summary of Awards

POKE London had a massive run of Webby Award recognition in the mid-to-late 2000s.
The agency specifically notes almost 20 Webby Nominations and Wins over a 10 year period.

## Specific Project Awards

1. **Orange: Balloonacy**
   - Won the 2009 Webby Award Winner
   - Category: Advertising Media / Individual Categories / Game or Application.
   - Also noted as 2008 People's Voice Winner (Orange general)
   - MediaGuardian Innovation Awards (The Megas): won "Digital Creative" in 2009.

2. **Kate Moss Topshop**
   - The Topshop brand as a whole (handled digitally by POKE) won the 2007 Webby Award.
   - Category: Web / General Website / Fashion
   - Simon Waterfall (POKE founder) is credited with driving the Kate Moss at Topshop brand identity online.

3. **Other Major POKE Accolades (Context)**
   - Alexander McQueen website (2005 Webby)
   - Jamie Oliver website (2005 Webby)
   - Manchester City Football Club (2010 Webby)
   - BakerTweet (2010 Nominee)
   - MoMA (2012 People's Voice Winner)
