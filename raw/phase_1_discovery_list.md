# Phase 1: Research Discovery (Iain Tait)

This document contains the initial broad sweep of research regarding Iain Tait's career, gathered to prioritize which targets the agents should ingest deeply into the wiki.

## 1. Wieden+Kennedy London (2014-2021)

### Nike: "Nothing Beats a Londoner" (2018)

- **Role:** Executive Creative Director (alongside Tony Davidson)
- **Context:** Grand Prix / Titanium Lion winning campaign highlighting real London youth, Olympians (Mo Farah), and musicians (Skepta, J Hus) in a hyper-paced one-upmanship film.
- **Leads:** D&AD archive, Shots.net, Creativepool case studies.

### Formula One: Global Rebrand (2017)

- **Role:** Executive Creative Director
- **Context:** Massive global overhaul of the F1 brand, modernizing it as an entertainment entity. W+K continued to produce campaigns like "Engineered Insanity" (2018). (Note: Richard Turley is often heavily cited alongside him here; interesting crossover with FOOD later).

### The Guggenheim / Gillian Wearing: "Wearing, Gillian" (2018)

- **Role:** Executive Creative Director
- **Context:** Deepfake integration into fine art. Face-swapping tech mapping actors to Gillian Wearing's features. Explored AI, artificiality, and self.
- **Leads:** W+K archive, Regen Projects, art galleries (Cincinnati Art Museum / Guggenheim New York).

## 2. Wieden+Kennedy Portland (2010-2012)

### Old Spice: "The Man Your Man Could Smell Like" (Responses)

- **Role:** Global Interactive Executive Creative Director
- **Context:** Led the "Responses" campaign (Isaiah Mustafa responding in near real-time on social media). Frequently cited as a landmark digital interaction and social media watershed moment.
- **Leads:** Forbes, FastCompany, marketing study texts.

## 3. POKE London (2000-2010)

- **Role:** Co-founder
- **Context:** Iconic early digital agency.
- **Notable Mention:** "Baker Tweet" (Albion cafe hardware/Twitter integration).
- **Leads:** The Drum, Campaign Live retrospectives. (Finding granular case studies here will be harder as it requires digging into historical web archives).

## 4. FOOD (Food Arts & Technology) (2022-Present)

- **Role:** Founding Partner (alongside Nick Farnhill and Richard Turley)
- **Context:** Transatlantic studio (London/NYC) fighting duplicative agency models. Blends AI/tech (Iain) with culture/design (Richard).
- **Clients:** Mulberry, Superblue, Semafor, RockCorp.
- **Leads:** thesubtext.online, Campaign Live interviews.
