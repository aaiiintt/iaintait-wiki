## 1. Nexus Interactive Arts — Relationship to Hellicar & Lewis

### Key Finding: Two Separate Entities Working Together

Nexus Interactive Arts and Hellicar & Lewis are **distinct organisations** that collaborated on the Coca-Cola / Maroon 5 "24hr Session" project. They are not one entity. The confusion arises because press releases and Iain Tait's own Campaign write-up use the shorthand "Nexus Interactive Arts/Hellicar and Lewis" — a slash notation indicating a joint partnership on the specific brief, not an organisational merger.

**Evidence:**

- **The Drum (March 2011):** "The real-time visuals will be entrusted to **Nexus Interactive Arts** directing duo **Hellicar&Lewis**." — This phrasing suggests Hellicar & Lewis operated *through* or *as* Nexus Interactive Arts' directing roster for this project.

- **Iain Tait, Campaign magazine (April 2011):** "We took this challenge to **Nexus Interactive Arts/Hellicar and Lewis**, and worked with them to create a huge projection-mapped sculpture in the middle of the studio."

- **Hellicar & Lewis portfolio site (`lessold.hellicarandlewis.com`):** "Hellicar&Lewis teamed up with **Wieden+Kennedy** and **Nexus Interactive Arts**, to create a 24 hour broadcast event for Coca Cola featuring Maroon 5." — Here they are listed as separate collaborators.

- **Hellicar & Lewis older blog (`old.hellicarandlewis.com`, posted March 22, 2011):** "On March 22nd, 2011, Hellicar&Lewis teamed up with **Nexus Productions**, Wieden+Kennedy, Frukt, Lexus PR, Coca-Cola and Maroon 5..." — notably uses "Nexus Productions" not "Nexus Interactive Arts."

- **MarkLives.com (March 2012, interview with Hellicar & Lewis):** "In March 2011 Hellicar & Lewis teamed up with **Nexus Productions**, Wieden+Kennedy, Frukt, Lexus PR, Coca-Cola and Maroon 5 to put on a live streamed 24 hour music event."

### What this means

**Nexus Interactive Arts** (later known as **Nexus Productions** / **Nexus Studios**) is a London-based production and interactive arts studio. They were the **production/technology partner** on the project. **Hellicar & Lewis** (Pete Hellicar + Joel Gethin Lewis) is an independent **interaction design duo** who directed the visual identity and created the gestural openFrameworks system. The duo were either represented by Nexus Interactive Arts for this commission, or the two entities were credited jointly as co-delivering the interactive system.

The terminology drifts across sources: "Nexus Interactive Arts" and "Nexus Productions" are used interchangeably — likely referring to the same company at different stages of its naming. The company is now trading as **Nexus Studios** (nexusstudios.com) and is an award-winning global studio in film and experience design (Emmy winner, BAFTA winner, Cannes Lions winner).

### Hellicar & Lewis: Who are they?

- **Pete Hellicar** — co-founder of Unabomber Skateboards (1997), later Art Director at Etnies
- **Joel Gethin Lewis** — interaction designer, formerly at United Visual Artists; worked at Dazed & Confused and AnotherMag.com
- Partnership: Hellicar & Lewis (hellicarandlewis.com)
- Primary tool: openFrameworks (open source C++ toolkit for creative coding)
- Philosophy: open source, physical/digital integration, no instruction manuals for installations
- The Coke 24hr Music project used openFrameworks to create gestural projection-mapping allowing the band to interact with fan tweets in real time
- Technical collaborators on the Coke project: Chris Sugrue, James George, Josh Nimoy, Joshua Noble, Marek Bereza, Todd Vanderlin
- Camera direction: Tomas Leach
- Live streaming: Streaming Tank + Akamai

### Coca-Cola Role — What We Know

No specific job title for her Coca-Cola role has been confirmed in available sources. She is listed alongside Ogilvy as part of her early career foundation. The Drum/Campaign coverage of the March 2011 Maroon 5 project identifies the Coca-Cola client contact as **Joe Belliotti, Director of Global Entertainment Marketing** — so Jantos may have been a separate marketing contact at Coca-Cola for this initiative, or she may have been at Ogilvy or another role at the time. Her own bio says she has worked "at companies such as The Coca-Cola Company and Ogilvy" — the sequence and exact titles require further research.

**Note for wiki:** The claim that Jackie Jantos was specifically the "client contact on the Coca-Cola/Maroon 5 project (W+K Portland, 2011)" should be treated as provisional until confirmed by primary documentation. Joe Belliotti is the named Coca-Cola contact in the press release. Jantos's Coca-Cola period is confirmed but her 2011 role/title is not yet verified.

## Summary: Key Decisions for Wiki

1. **Nexus Interactive Arts** = production/technology company (now trading as Nexus Studios). **Hellicar & Lewis** = separate interaction design duo (Pete Hellicar + Joel Gethin Lewis). They were joint delivery partners on the Coke 24hr project; Hellicar & Lewis was likely represented through/alongside Nexus Interactive Arts for this commission. File both as distinct collaborators.

2. **Jackie Jantos**: Her Hinge CEO appointment (December 9, 2025) is confirmed. Career includes Coca-Cola, Ogilvy, Spotify (VP Global Brand & Creative, 2014–2020 approx), Dashlane (CMO), Hinge (CMO 2021 → President March 2025 → CEO December 2025). Her specific Coca-Cola title and exact years are unconfirmed in open sources. Her connection to the 2011 Maroon 5 project as client contact is not yet independently verified in press coverage — Joe Belliotti is the named Coke contact.

3. **Dom Felton**: Executive Producer at W+K London since November 2010 (started as Producer/Senior Producer). Confirmed on Ingress Prime (2018) as co-Producer. Confirmed as Executive Producer on Malibu (2024). His W+K London tenure is long-running and covers the full period of the Niantic projects. Background: Ogilvy Interactive, Holler, Unilever.
