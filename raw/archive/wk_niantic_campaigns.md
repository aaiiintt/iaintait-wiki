## Project: Pokémon GO "City Celebrations" (1-Year Anniversary)

### Client: Niantic

### Agency: Wieden+Kennedy London

- **Launched**: June/July 2017
- **Overview**: A global outdoor and digital marketing campaign to celebrate the one-year anniversary of the game.
- **Key Executions**:
  - Focused on top "trainer cities" like London, New York, and Tokyo.
  - Featured real-time statistics regarding local gameplay (e.g., number of Pokémon caught in specific cities).
  - **Innovative Mechanics**: The physical OOH billboards were designated as in-game PokéStops, blurring the line between advertising and gameplay.
- **Leadership / Iain's Involvement**: Iain Tait, as ECD of Wieden+Kennedy London, noted the agency's admiration for Niantic's mission to inspire real-world exploration through gaming.
- **Coordination**: W+K London led creative; W+K Portland handled global media planning and buying.

## Project: Harry Potter: Wizards Unite Global Launch

### Client: Niantic / Warner Bros

### Agency: Wieden+Kennedy London

- **Launched**: June 2019
- **Overview**: A global campaign to launch the augmented reality game *Harry Potter: Wizards Unite*.
- **Narrative**: Treated the game narrative as a real-world event—"The Calamity"—where magic uncontrollably appears in the Muggle world. The Ministry of Magic recruits players to stop it.
- **Executions**:
  - Early teaser films pushed to social channels since January 2019 showing "traces of magic" caught on CCTV.
  - "The Trace Report" directed by William McGregor and Jorge Montiel.
  - Main launch film (released June 19, 2019) directed by Tom Green, produced by Stink and The Mill. Follows wizards-in-training defending a young Harry from Dementors.
  - Rolled out globally across TV, Cinema, Online, Social, and significant Out-of-Home press activity.
  - Marked Niantic's largest media investment to date.
