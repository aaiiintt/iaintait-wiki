## Agency: Wieden+Kennedy London

### Project: "Helping Everyone Eat Better"

- **Client**: Sainsbury's
- **Year**: 2021 (May)
- **Overview**: A major brand realignment and advertising campaign that fundamentally replaced the supermarket's 10-year-old slogan "Live Well for Less" with "Helping Everyone Eat Better".
- **Strategic Purpose**: Aimed to encourage consumers to make health-conscious and environmentally sustainable food choices, aligning closely with the UK government's "Eatwell Guide" and advocating a "One Plate at a Time" mentality.
- **Key Execution**: A 60-second television commercial, voiced by Stephen Fry, featuring creative visual transitions (e.g., framing produce to look like sprawling physical landscapes). Directed by Filip Nilsson and produced by Object & Animal.
- **Brand Impact**: The campaign extended beyond conventional marketing, pushing internal systemic changes at Sainsbury’s, such as reformulating own-brand items to reduce salt/sugar, and publishing goals for net-zero carbon and plastic reduction.
