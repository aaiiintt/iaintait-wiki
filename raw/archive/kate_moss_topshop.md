Agency: POKE London
Year: 2007

## Content

POKE (also known as Poke London) was responsible for creating the digital brand identity and visual identity for the high-profile "Kate Moss at Topshop" collaboration.

This was a landmark digital campaign (~2007) to support the original launch of the Kate Moss collection for Topshop, which became a landmark moment in high-street fashion history.

POKE built a highly anticipated, two-phase interactive lookbook. It captured the massive cultural moment (where fans literally queued overnight on Oxford Street) and translated that hype into an online experience. Topshop's collaborations with Kate Moss were transformative for high-street fashion, and POKE's digital execution matched the exclusivity and highly sought-after nature of the physical release.
