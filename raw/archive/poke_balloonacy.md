# Source: Advertising Archives / Memory

Date: 2008
Brand: Orange UK
Agency: POKE London

## Content

"Orange Balloonacy" was launched in June 2008 as a digital campaign for Orange UK (specifically for their "Orange Animals Pay As You Go" offering). It was famously dubbed "The world's first internet balloon race".

The campaign allowed users to create and fly personalized balloons across the internet. The virtual balloons traversed across 1,500 different websites (including major portals like Yahoo and The Sun). A widget on these sites allowed the balloons to 'float' across the content.

Stats:

- 30,000 balloons took part in the race.
- The winning balloon was named "Reginald Ringtail the Raccoon".
- The winner covered 5,033 internet miles.

This was an early and highly innovative use of cross-site digital widgets to create a persistent, shared multiplayer experience for an advertising campaign.
