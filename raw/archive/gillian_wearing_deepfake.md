## Agency: Wieden+Kennedy London

### Project: "Wearing Gillian" (Gillian Wearing Deepfake)

- **Collaborator**: Gillian Wearing (Turner Prize-winning artist)
- **Year**: 2018
- **Overview**: A collaborative film titled *Wearing Gillian*, created for her exhibition *Life: Gillian Wearing* at the Cincinnati Art Museum.
- **Technical Execution**: The film utilized highly advanced deepfake (face-swapping) AI technology to permanently map Wearing's facial features onto a series of actors and volunteers forming a cohesive narrative.
- **Production Hook**: Produced in collaboration with Wieden+Kennedy London, executing the deepfake pipeline alongside production company Private Island.
- **Artistic Intent**: Wearing is historically known for using physical masks to explore themes of identity and performance. The project recognized deepfake AI as the ultimate modern, digital extension of her legacy practice—allowing the public to "wear" her identity without silicone.
- **Legacy**: Posed early critical questions regarding the artificiality of identity in the modern media landscape using a bleeding-edge commercial AI toolkit.
