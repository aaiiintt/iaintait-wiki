# Music Response

## Overview

"Music Response" is an experiential production delivered by FOOD for the legendary electronic music duo, The Chemical Brothers.

## Project Summary

As part of the agency's portfolio exploring the intersection of culture, art, and technology, this project involved developing cutting-edge, immersive experiential components for the band, known globally for their boundary-pushing audio-visual live performances.
