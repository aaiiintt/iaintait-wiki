# Sainsbury's: Helping Everyone Eat Better

## The Campaign

A major brand platform shift — retiring Sainsbury's decade-long "Live Well for Less" slogan and launching **"Helping Everyone Eat Better"**. The new platform repositioned Sainsbury's from a value proposition (price) to health, sustainability, and environmental responsibility.

Sainsbury's simultaneously announced aggressive internal targets: reduce single-use plastics, aim for net-zero carbon, reformulate own-brand products for less salt and sugar. The campaign was the public face of a deeper operational realignment.

## The Work

A 60-second TV spot using **match-cut cinematography** to frame raw produce and ingredients as sprawling geographical landscapes — collapsing the visual distance between the food on your plate and the environment it came from. Narrated by **Stephen Fry**. The message: "One Plate at a Time" — small, sustainable changes rather than preachy dietary demands.

**3.2 million YouTube views** on Sainsbury's channel.
