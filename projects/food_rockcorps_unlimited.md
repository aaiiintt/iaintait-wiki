# RockCorps Unlimited

## Overview

A continuous creative collaboration spanning entertainment and cultural impact for RockCorps.

## Project Summary

RockCorps is a pro-social production company that uses music to inspire volunteerism ("Give, Get Given"). FOOD partnered with RockCorps Unlimited post-2022 to innovate their format, modernize their digital cultural footprint, and develop new entertainment models that blend high-profile music events with scalable social impact programs for modern audiences.
