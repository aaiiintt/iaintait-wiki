## Bud Light: "Keep It Bud Light" (2017)

**First UK campaign for the brand.**

### Phase 1 — OOH Launch (March 2017)
National billboard rollout including the London IMAX and Birmingham Media Eyes. Tagline: **"Keep It Bud Light"**. Established the brand's visual identity across UK outdoor.

### Phase 2 — TV (October 2017)
A parody lifestyle ad — Bud Light playing with the conventions of premium beer advertising. Directed by **Ali Ali** through **Sonny London** — his first UK ad. (Ali Ali is the acclaimed Egyptian commercial director.)

## Tyrrells: "Absurdly Good" (September 2017)

**First-ever television campaign for the brand.** Budget: £2.5m.

### The Work
Two spots launched 11 September 2017, running through to end of November:
- **"Very Important Potatoes"** — 20" TVC
- **"Fabulously Curly"** — 20" social film

Voiceover by **Peter Serafinowicz**. Maintained Tyrrells' distinctly eccentric, vintage aesthetic while scaling it to national television.
