# Nano Banana / Gemini R&D

## Overview

An expansive R&D project partnering with Google to demonstrate the raw creative potential of the Gemini AI model to global brands.

## Project Summary

Functioning as rapid, high-fidelity innovation sprints, this project involved taking "Nano Banana" and scaling it into a viable cultural phenomenon using generative AI. By pushing Gemini to its creative limits, the FOOD team developed prototypes, creative workflows, and proof-of-concept activations to showcase how mega-brands like LEGO and IKEA could authentically leverage the LLM for brand building, product design, and interactive storytelling.
