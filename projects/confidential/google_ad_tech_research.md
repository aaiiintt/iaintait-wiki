# Google Ad-Tech Research (Keeper Application)

## Overview

Internal Google Creative Lab R&D exploring advertising UX and content personalisation, resulting in US Patent US20170337584A1.

## Patents & Intellectual Property

The conceptual framework built during this internal research sprint resulted in a US Patent granted to Google:

* **[US20170337584A1: Systems and methods for serving secondary online content based on interactions with primary online content and concierge rules](https://patents.google.com/patent/US20170337584A1/en)**
  * **Inventors:** William Davison Najdecki, Iain Tait, Andrew Ackermann, Michael Gerard Morrissey, Gabriel Oak Taubman, Jason Sanders
  * **Filed:** July 23, 2014 (Priority Date: April 30, 2014)
  * **Publication Date:** November 23, 2017
