# Dior Addict / Gaia

## Overview

A multi-sensory brand campaign for Dior targeting the Gen Z demographic.

## Project Summary

This project leveraged FOOD's expertise to bridge heritage luxury with modern digital behaviors. The campaign theme orbited around the concept of "joyful chaos," utilizing AI-assisted content pipelines to generate highly shareable, visually disruptive assets. It demonstrated how classical high fashion brands could deploy advanced technology to resonate with younger, digitally native audiences without compromising their prestige identity.
