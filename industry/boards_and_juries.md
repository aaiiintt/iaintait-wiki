## Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity

### [Cyber Lions — Jury President (2012)](talks/cannes_cyber_lions_jury_president.md)
The most significant single jury appointment. Named Jury President of the Cannes Cyber Lions in February 2012, while Global Interactive ECD at Wieden+Kennedy — and presided at the ceremony in June 2012 by which point he had joined Google Creative Lab. His jury awarded the Grand Prix to Nike+ FuelBand and Curators of Sweden.

- **Announced:** February 3, 2012
- **Affiliation (announced):** Global Interactive ECD, Wieden+Kennedy
- **Affiliation (ceremony):** ECD, Google Creative Lab
- **Grand Prix winners announced:** Nike+ FuelBand · Curators of Sweden
- **Sources:** [Fast Company, June 20, 2012](https://www.fastcompany.com/1680967/nike-fuelband-and-curators-of-sweden-take-top-cyber-lions-at-cannes) · [LBBOnline, April 5, 2012](https://lbbonline.com/news/cannes-lions-announces-three-further-juries) · [AdHugger, Feb 3, 2012](https://www.adhugger.net/2012/02/03/david-jones-iain-tait-and-mainardo-de-nardis-to-head-juries-at-cannes-lions-2012/) · [PRNewswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/usa-today-announces-us-jurors-for-2012-cannes-lions-international-festival-of-creativity-141901333.html)

> *"Both pieces are about brands behaving a certain way. They're not about what they say and noisy messages, they're about what brands do and that's what we're excited by."* — Iain Tait, announcing the Cyber Grand Prix, Cannes 2012

### Titanium & Integrated — Jury Member (2011)
- **Affiliation at time:** Global Interactive ECD, Wieden+Kennedy
- **Sources:** [Ad Age (June 25, 2011)](https://adage.com/article/creativity-cannes-2011/droga-5-s-decode-jay-z-bing-wins-time-integrated-grand-prix/228435/) · [AdNews AU (June 26, 2011)](https://www.adnews.com.au/adnews/get-over-facebook-twitter-say-titanium-and-integrated-cannes-jury)

## International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences (IADAS)

### IADAS Member / Lovie Awards Juror (2018–present)
As an inducted IADAS member, Iain is part of the voting body that identifies digital excellence globally. Confirmed as active member during W+K London tenure (2014–2021) and beyond.

- **Confirmed years:** 2018 (8th Lovie Awards), 2020 (10th Lovie Awards)
- **Sources:** [8th Annual Lovie Awards press release](https://lovieawards.eu/jung-von-matt-wins-agency-of-the-year-at-the-8th-annual-lovie-awards/) · [10th Annual Lovie Awards reference](https://other.media/ospreys-app-shortlisted-for-design-award/)

### The Webby Awards
As an IADAS member, Iain serves as an executive judge for the Webby Awards — the leading international award honouring excellence on the internet.
