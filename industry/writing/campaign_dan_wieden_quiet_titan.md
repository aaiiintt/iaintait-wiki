# Dan Wieden: the quiet titan

**Publication:** Campaign
**Date:** 2022-10-06
**Author:** Iain Tait

## Synopsis

Iain Tait's personal tribute to Dan Wieden, published in Campaign following Wieden's death on September 30, 2022. Dan Wieden was co-founder of Wieden+Kennedy — the largest independently owned advertising agency in the world — and the creator of Nike's "Just Do It" tagline.

This is a deeply personal piece. Dan Wieden personally recruited Iain to W+K Portland in 2010, championed his promotion to partner in 2011, and continued as a mentor through Iain's years as ECD of W+K London (2014–2021). Wieden famously described the agency's culture as one built on "walking into the room and finding the smartest person there" — and by recruiting Iain from POKE, he signalled that digital-native thinking belonged at the heart of the agency, not at its margins.

The piece in Campaign was one of several tributes from W+K alumni and the broader industry following Wieden's death.
