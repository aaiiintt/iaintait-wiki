# Publications

Iain Tait's influence extends deeply into trade press and industry journalism. Over the decades, his written opinions, agency profiles, and tech predictions have been extensively featured.

## Own Writing

* **[Dan Wieden: the quiet titan (Campaign, 2022)](writing/campaign_dan_wieden_quiet_titan.md):** Iain's personal tribute to Dan Wieden, published following Wieden's death. A significant primary source on the Tait–Wieden relationship.

## Trade Press Coverage & Interviews

* **[Creative Review: Creative Leaders on Beginnings (2019)](interviews/creative_review_beginnings.md):** Career origins interview as part of CR's leadership series.
* **[Ad Age: Iain Tait Leaves W+K for Google (2012)](interviews/ad_age_leaves_wk_for_google.md):** Coverage of the W+K → Google Creative Lab move.
* **[W+K Partner Announcement (2011)](interviews/wk_partner_announcement.md):** Promotion to partner, with Dan Wieden quote.
* **[Boards Magazine: Joining W+K (2010)](interviews/boards_joining_wk.md):** Interview about the POKE → W+K transition.
* **Campaign Magazine (UK/Global):** Iain is frequently sourced as a primary voice regarding digital disruption in traditional advertising.
* **The Drum:** Coverage spans his time co-founding POKE to his departure from W+K to start FOOD.
* **It's Nice That and AdAge:** Widespread coverage regarding his creative leadership philosophy and insights into major brand maneuvers (such as Nike, Formula 1, and Google).

*(Note: Because of his high profile as a Global ECD, much of Iain's thought leadership is captured via interviews and strategic quotes within these outlets rather than dedicated column space—aside from his historic tenure writing for New Media Age).*
