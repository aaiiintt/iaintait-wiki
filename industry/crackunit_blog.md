# Crackunit & Crackunit2

Long before "thought leadership" became ubiquitous on platforms like LinkedIn, Iain Tait authored **Crackunit** (and its successor, **Crackunit2**).

This highly regarded industry blog ran throughout the late 2000s and was frequently cited alongside seminal digital publications like BBH Labs and Adliterate. It served as one of the premier digital marketing and planning blogs in the UK.

## The Format & Impact

The blog was known for blending traditional advertising planning and best practices with a distinct, often skeptical, digital and anthropological perspective. It became a required reading reference point for digital strategists navigating the transition from Web 1.0 to social platforms and physical computing, offering unfiltered, pragmatic, and insightful takes on the rapidly evolving digital landscape.
