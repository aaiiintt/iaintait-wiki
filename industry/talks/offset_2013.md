# OFFSET 2013

**Event:** OFFSET Conference, Dublin
**Year:** 2013 (April)
**Speaker:** Iain Tait
**Affiliation:** [Google Creative Lab](../../agencies/google_creative_lab.md)

## Synopsis

Iain appeared at OFFSET, Dublin's premier creative conference, in April 2013. At the time, he was serving as Executive Creative Director at Google Creative Lab (2012–2014). Speaking to an audience of 2,000 creatives at the Bord Gáis Energy Theatre, Iain's presentation centered on the philosophy of **"Hacking"** — urging the audience to "use and shape what's around you to create great things" rather than waiting for permission or structured pipelines.

## The Bob Gill Jumper Incident

This session is immortalized in OFFSET festival lore not just for the talk itself, but for the Q&A panel interactions. The legendary rebel designer and D&AD co-founder **Bob Gill** took a pointed, playful public swipe at Iain Tait's choice of jumper (sweater). The same lively Q&A session also featured Bob Gill getting into a famous "barney" (heated debate) with illustrator Kate Moross, making it one of the most animated and memorable panels in the conference's history.
