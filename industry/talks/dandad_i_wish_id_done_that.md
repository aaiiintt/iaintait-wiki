# D&AD: I Wish I'd Done That

**Event:** D&AD
**Year:** 2013 (November)
**Participants:** Dan Wieden, Iain Tait, Jessica Walsh

## Synopsis

A D&AD film in which three prominent creatives — Dan Wieden (co-founder of Wieden+Kennedy), Iain Tait, and Jessica Walsh (then of Sagmeister & Walsh) — discuss work created by others that they wish they had made. The format invites vulnerability and generosity: creatives admitting admiration for peers' work, revealing what they value in the craft.

The pairing with Dan Wieden is particularly significant given their working relationship — Iain was a partner at W+K Portland at the time, and Wieden personally recruited him. This film predates Iain's return to W+K London as ECD.

## Collaborators

- **Dan Wieden** — W+K co-founder. Mentor and collaborator.
- **Jessica Walsh** — Designer, then at Sagmeister & Walsh.
