# Wieden+Kennedy Partner Announcement

**Source:** Wieden+Kennedy / Trade Press
**Date:** December 2011
**Subject:** Iain Tait promoted to Partner at W+K Portland

## Synopsis

Wieden+Kennedy announced Iain Tait as a partner of the agency in December 2011, approximately 18 months after he joined from POKE London. The announcement included a quote from Dan Wieden about Iain — a notable personal endorsement from the agency's founder.

Being named partner at W+K — the world's largest independent advertising agency — was a significant milestone, confirming that a creative leader who came up through digital agencies could earn a seat at the top table of mainstream advertising.

This promotion came just months before Iain would leave for Google Creative Lab (April 2012), making his W+K Portland tenure relatively brief but intensely impactful — encompassing [Old Spice Responses](../../projects/wk_old_spice_responses.md) and [Nike Better World](../../projects/wk_nike_better_world.md).
