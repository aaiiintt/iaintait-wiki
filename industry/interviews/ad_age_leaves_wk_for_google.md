# Ad Age: Iain Tait Leaves W+K for Google

**Publication:** Ad Age
**Date:** April 2012
**Subject:** Career move from W+K Portland to Google Creative Lab

## Synopsis

Ad Age coverage of Iain Tait's move from Wieden+Kennedy Portland to Google Creative Lab in New York. This was major trade press news — a high-profile creative leaving one of the world's most celebrated agencies for a tech company's internal creative team.

The move signalled a broader industry trend: top advertising talent being recruited by technology companies who were building their own creative capabilities in-house. Google Creative Lab under Robert Wong was assembling an unusually creative team, and Iain's recruitment (alongside figures like [James Cooper](../../collaborators/james_cooper.md)) gave the Lab credibility as a destination for elite agency talent.
