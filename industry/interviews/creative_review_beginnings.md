# Creative Review: Creative Leaders on Beginnings

**Publication:** Creative Review
**Date:** January 2019 (approx)
**Format:** Interview / profile series
**Affiliation:** [Wieden+Kennedy London](../../agencies/wieden_and_kennedy_london.md) (ECD)

## Synopsis

Creative Review interview with Iain Tait as part of their "Creative Leaders on Beginnings" series, discussing his career origins. This format typically asks senior creatives to reflect on their formative experiences, early influences, and the path that led them to their current positions.

During this period Iain was ECD at W+K London, overseeing campaigns for Nike, Three, Formula 1, and others.
