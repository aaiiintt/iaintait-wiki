# Boards Magazine: Joining Wieden+Kennedy

**Publication:** Boards Magazine
**Date:** March 2010 (approx)
**Subject:** Iain Tait's move from POKE London to W+K Portland

## Synopsis

Interview in Boards magazine about Iain Tait joining Wieden+Kennedy Portland, leaving POKE London — the agency he co-founded a decade earlier. The piece discusses the motivations for the move and references the PSFK ["10 Reasons Why Digital is Better than Advertising"](../talks/psfk_10_reasons_digital.md) talk as a pivotal moment that set Iain on the path from digital specialist to broader creative leadership.

This interview is significant as a primary source documenting the POKE → W+K transition, one of the defining career moves in Iain's trajectory.
