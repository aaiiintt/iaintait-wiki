## Working with Iain & FOOD

### How do I work with Iain?
Iain works at FOOD, so all commercial and consulting enquiries are routed through the consultancy. Depending on the project, FOOD often operates as an outside creative provocateur or creative lab to show the potential of what is possible using creativity and new technologies.

### What is Iain's current focus?
Right now, Iain is focusing on helping people figure out what AI means for creativity—and applying that to modern marketing tasks, combined with the deep creative heritage detailed in this archive.

### What kinds of engagement models do you offer?
We offer flexible structures depending on the nature of the challenge:
* **Two-week intense sprints**
* **Three-month prototyping labs**
* **Ongoing retainers**
* Custom creative and strategic consulting arrangements.

### Do you work remotely or on-site?
We are happy to work remotely and frequently do. However, we love having our physical studios in London and New York because they give us spaces to hang out with friends and collaborate together.

### How does FOOD scale to handle large production or specialist tasks?
We keep our core team small and lean, but we work with a super-talented network of industry veterans and bright young talent. We also maintain an established network of partner companies for specialist production, development, and execution.

### Will FOOD pitch for our business?
No, FOOD does not pitch. We are a small, lean consultancy and do not have the capacity for unpaid pitch processes. We will happily meet with potential partners to see if we gel creatively, or work on a paid "taster" project to test the relationship, but doing creative work for free is silly.

### What is the day rate or pricing?
Rates and pricing depend entirely on the specific assignment and scope of the work.

### Are you open to equity or joint venture deals?
Yes, we do these often and are highly open to equity conversations. For example, we established a joint venture with The Chemical Brothers and Smith & Lyall for our **[Music Response](../projects/food_chemical_brothers_music_response.md)** project, along with other key partnerships.
