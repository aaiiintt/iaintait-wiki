# Creative Review: Creativity Sucks! — AI Discussion

**Podcast:** Creativity Sucks! (Creative Review)
**Date:** May 2023 (approx)
**Guest:** Iain Tait
**Affiliation:** FOOD

## Synopsis

Iain Tait appears on Creative Review's "Creativity Sucks!" podcast discussing AI and creativity. This was an early and prominent industry conversation about generative AI's impact on the creative professions, arriving in the wake of ChatGPT's launch (November 2022) and the rapid proliferation of AI image generators.

Creative Review is one of the UK's most respected creative industry publications, so this represents an authoritative platform for Iain's perspective on AI — a subject that would become central to FOOD's practice.
