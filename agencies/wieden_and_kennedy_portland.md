# Wieden+Kennedy Portland

## Agency Overview

The Portland headquarters of Wieden+Kennedy is iconic in the advertising industry, historically renowned for its broadcast output and defining the voice of Nike. Between 2010 and 2012, the agency underwent a significant digital transformation.

## Iain Tait's Role

Iain Tait joined Wieden+Kennedy Portland as Global Interactive Executive Creative Director. His mandate was to elevate the agency's digital thinking to match its broadcast pedigree. Under his leadership, the interactive work achieved global cultural penetration, culminating in the first Digital Grand Prix for the agency.
