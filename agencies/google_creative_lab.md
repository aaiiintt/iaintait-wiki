# Google Creative Lab (NYC)

## Agency Overview

Google Creative Lab is an internal think tank and creative studio tasked with exploring the boundaries of Google's technology and brand. During the 2012–2014 period, the Lab produced a staggering array of "Chrome Experiments" designed to demonstrate the potential of the modern open web, blurring the lines between mobile and desktop interaction without the need for native applications.

## Iain Tait's Role

Serving at the NYC office (2012-2014), Iain drove the conceptualization and production of highly visible, technically ambitious browser experiences and large-scale consumer campaigns.
