# Wieden+Kennedy London

## Agency Overview

Wieden+Kennedy is a preeminent global independent creative agency network. The London office has produced some of the world's most culturally penetrative and highly-awarded work for clients like Nike, Honda, and Formula One.

## Iain Tait's Role

Iain Tait served as Executive Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy London (and previously held digital leadership roles at the agency), where he helped steer the agency's creative output across a massive roster of global brands. A hallmark of his tenure was embracing digital, physical, and gaming intersectionality.
